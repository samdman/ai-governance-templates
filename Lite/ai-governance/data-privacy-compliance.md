# Data Privacy & Compliance — Lite

## Required
- Classify sensitive and PII fields used by the service.
- Do not log PII or sensitive payloads.
- Define retention period for operational data and logs.

## Recommended
- Support deletion/anonymization workflows where required.
- Restrict access to sensitive data by role.

## Forbid
- Retaining sensitive data without business/legal justification.
- Broad access to PII without need-to-know controls.
